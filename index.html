FACTS ABOUT COLONIES

Colonies is a platform that is created to raise fund for building the first prototype of Spacetrain, a modern spaceship that converts spring potential energy into kinetic energy to propel itself using minimal electricity.

I currently need to raise only $2,000 to pay for patent filing and put the blueprint of the spaceship into illustration videos for critics and potential investors to view and understand the concept of the Spacetrain. If this community were to have a million active users, one month should be enough to raise $2,000 from ads which will lead to next step of raising fund for a prototype from investors.

Colonies consists of two tokens : Spacecoin and Spacepoints. Both are stable tokens that will always remain a little value above USD. Spacepoints can only be used for national, international and interplanetary travel as well as spending on the e-commerce platform that will be integrated in the future. As soon as the first prototype of Spacetrain is built, a fraction of your Spacecoin will be converted into widthrawable cash as a reward for your participation in the community while the remaining fraction automatically converts to Spacepoints.

Unlike other platforms, Colonies has no complex or hidden airdrop requirements aimed deliberately at reducing the number of qualified users. I hate uncertainties and I don't want to bring it to the platform. Personally, I have tried mining in a few apps and even spend a little money but I never for once cash out because I usually don't meet up with all the criteria and I learned not to do similar here. Just play game on my mobile app and watch ads whenever you have the time to do so.
